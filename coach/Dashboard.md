## 🔧 System Status

- **Data Sync:** Every 4 hours  
- **Daily Coaching:** Every morning  
- **Weekly Summary:** Mondays  
- **Analysis + Plan:** Sundays  
- **LLM Provider:** Configurable (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Azure)
